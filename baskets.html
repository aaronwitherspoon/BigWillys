Setting up your own basket at home

What are the benefits of having your own basket?

Disc golf takes a lot of time and practice to become a better player. It isn't always feasible to travel to a disc golf course to get in some practice. It is much more economical to own your own basket and be able to practice at your convenience at home. There are commercial baskets that you can purchase or if you are frugal you can build your own. I can also custom build a basket for you. Please call if you are interested in a custom basket. Pricing will vary depending on your specific needs.
